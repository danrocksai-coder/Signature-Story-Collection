This Certified Photographer Partner Agreement supplements the Signature Story Collection Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. If this Agreement directly conflicts with the general Terms of Service, this Agreement controls for the approved Partner relationship. Partner approval, payment, electronic acceptance, or a signed copy may be used to confirm acceptance.

1. Parties and Purpose

This Agreement is between Signature Story Collection and the approved photography business identified in the Partner enrollment record (“Partner”).

The purpose of the relationship is to allow the Partner to offer clients a personalized custom-written song and cinematic photo story video created from the client’s story and the Partner’s finished professional photographs.

The Partner remains the customer-facing photography business. Signature Story Collection operates behind the scenes as the creative production provider.

2. Term and Renewal

The initial certification term begins on the date the applicable annual certification fee is received and continues for one year, unless earlier suspended or terminated under this Agreement.

Certification does not automatically renew unless automatic renewal is separately authorized. Signature Story Collection may contact the Partner before expiration with renewal instructions.

Renewal must be completed by the stated deadline to preserve certification, territory protection, and any grandfathered rate.

3. Protected Territory

One active Certified Partner per approved protected area.

Territory protection begins only after approval, acceptance, and payment. A territory request or verbal discussion does not reserve the area.

The approved territory may be defined by ZIP code, city, service area, or another written geographic description. The approved area will be recorded in the Partner’s enrollment confirmation.

Territory protection applies only to the Signature Story Collection Certified Photographer Partner Program. It does not prevent other photographers, song providers, video editors, or unrelated businesses from operating in the same market.

The Partner may serve clients outside the protected area, but marketing should not falsely claim exclusivity in an area assigned to another Partner. Additional territories require separate written approval.

A territory may be reopened if certification expires, payment is not completed, the Partner voluntarily leaves, the Agreement is terminated, or the territory remains inactive after the support and review process described below.

4. Certification and Partner Benefits

While active and in good standing, the Partner may receive:

  • Protected territory status for the approved area
  • A dedicated Partner landing page
  • A client story-submission process
  • A Certified Partner digital badge
  • A Founding Partner number or certificate when applicable
  • A personalized Partner-page QR code
  • Suggested pricing, scripts, launch copy, and promotional materials
  • Access to the published Certified Partner production rate
  • Photography credit on qualifying public collections
  • Eligibility for applicable Signature Rewards™ production credits
  • One complimentary standard collection during each active partnership year
  • Direct Partner support and available Partner Success sessions

Benefits may be delivered electronically and may be updated, replaced, expanded, or discontinued as the program develops. Signature Story Collection will not materially reduce an already paid core benefit without reasonable notice or an appropriate substitute.

5. Certification and Production Pricing

$25/year Founding Partners #1–100 while continuously eligible
$50 Standard Certified Partner production rate
1–20 Finished professional photographs per standard collection

The first 100 approved Partners may retain the $25 annual certification rate as long as they renew on time and remain in good standing. If certification expires, is canceled, or is deactivated, reentry is subject to current pricing and availability.

The standard production rate applies to the standard collection described in this Agreement. Advanced animation, extensive restoration, additional songs, major creative restarts, unusually complex edits, or other nonstandard work may require separate approval and pricing.

Signature Story Collection may change future pricing upon reasonable notice. A pricing change does not retroactively alter a completed project or a paid annual term unless required by law or agreed in writing.

6. Payment Terms

For the first five paid client projects, the standard production fee is due within 72 hours after delivery to the Partner unless another arrangement is confirmed in writing.

After five completed and successfully paid projects, a Partner in good standing may request Net 30 monthly billing. Net 30 approval is not automatic and may be suspended or revoked for late payment, failed payment, chargebacks, account concerns, or inactivity.

The Partner is responsible for applicable transaction charges, taxes, chargeback costs, and reasonable collection expenses when legally permitted.

Unpaid balances may delay new production, suspend access to Partner benefits, remove Net 30 privileges, pause the Partner page, or lead to termination.

7. Partner Sales and Client Responsibilities

The Partner independently decides:

  • When and how to offer Signature Story Collection
  • The retail price charged to the client
  • Whether to include the service in a photography package
  • The deposit, payment schedule, refund policy, and sales process
  • How to communicate with and deliver the finished collection to the client

The Partner keeps the amount collected from the client above the applicable Signature Story Collection production fee.

Suggested pricing and profit examples are educational only. No sales volume, income, profit, demand, or return on investment is guaranteed.

The Partner may not make guarantees, promises, legal commitments, or refund obligations on behalf of Signature Story Collection unless specifically authorized in writing.

8. Standard Client Process

  1. The Partner offers Signature Story Collection as a premium add-on, preferably at the end of the photography session while the client is still present.
  2. If the client accepts, the Partner sends the client the Partner’s dedicated Signature Story Collection page or submission link.
  3. The client submits names, memories, music direction, important details, publication preference, and anything to include or avoid.
  4. After the Partner finishes the normal editing process, the Partner has the client select between 1 and 20 finished photographs.
  5. The Partner submits the selected final images through the approved delivery method.
  6. Signature Story Collection creates the song, cinematic photo story video, captions, artwork, and digital delivery package.
  7. The completed collection is sent to the Partner for review and client delivery.

Production may be delayed until all required information, photographs, permissions, and payment arrangements are complete.

9. Photographs, Permissions, and Client Consent

The Partner represents that submitted photographs are original, licensed, or otherwise authorized for the requested production and publication selection.

The Partner is responsible for obtaining any client, model, parental, guardian, copyright, privacy, publicity, and publication permissions required for the project.

Submitted photographs remain the photographer’s work. Signature Story Collection will not intentionally recolor, retouch, filter, or change the photographer’s finished editing style. Minor cropping, scaling, or repositioning may be used when necessary to fit the video format.

Existing photographer logos, watermarks, and image signatures will remain visible when included in the submitted files.

10. Production Standards and AI-Assisted Creation

Signature Story Collection may use premium licensed software, including AI-assisted tools, for lyric development, music generation, editing, captioning, artwork, and production support. Human direction, editing, assembly, quality control, and final review remain part of the production process.

A standard collection generally includes:

  • One personalized custom-written song
  • One cinematic photo story video
  • Between 1 and 20 selected professional photographs
  • Clean fade transitions and intentional image timing
  • Readable lyric captions
  • A shareable YouTube link
  • Available HD video, MP3 audio, cover artwork, and lyric artwork

Creative work is subjective. Signature Story Collection will make a good-faith effort to follow the submitted story and requested direction, but no specific melody, vocal performance, emotional reaction, commercial result, or subjective response is guaranteed.

11. Review, Revisions, and Delivery

One reasonable revision round is included with each standard collection for corrections or limited changes such as:

  • A misspelled name or incorrect date
  • A limited lyric or caption correction
  • A reasonable image substitution
  • A minor timing or sequencing adjustment

A complete rewrite, replacement song, new genre, major restructuring, extensive image changes, or work caused by omitted or incorrect information may require an additional fee or a new project.

The Partner should review the package promptly and provide one clear, consolidated revision request. Delivery timeframes are estimates unless a deadline is confirmed in writing.

The Partner is responsible for downloading and preserving delivered files. Permanent storage and indefinite availability of third-party links are not guaranteed.

12. YouTube Publication and Photographer Credit

Finished collections are delivered through the official Signature Story Collection YouTube channel.

Public publication is the standard selection when disclosed and authorized through the applicable client process. A client who prefers greater privacy must select the unlisted option before production begins.

An unlisted video may be viewed or shared by anyone with the direct link. No internet-based delivery method guarantees complete secrecy.

Public collections may include:

  • The Partner’s photography business name
  • The Partner’s website and service area
  • Certified Partner status
  • A photography credit end card
  • Existing logos, watermarks, or image signatures

Exposure, views, subscribers, leads, search placement, and audience growth are not guaranteed.

13. Marketing, Badge Use, and Brand Standards

While active and in good standing, the Partner receives a limited, revocable, nonexclusive right to use approved Certified Partner badges, marketing materials, descriptions, and promotional assets solely to market the Partner’s authorized participation.

The Partner may not alter the Signature Story Collection name, badge, logo, claims, pricing, guarantees, or legal language in a way that is misleading, damaging, or inconsistent with the program.

The Partner may not register or use a confusingly similar domain, social handle, business name, trademark, or advertising identity.

Certified Partner materials must be removed when certification expires, is suspended, or is terminated.

14. Signature Rewards™ and Production Credits

Monthly paid collection totals may qualify the Partner for production credits under the published Signature Rewards structure.

  • 5 completed paid collections: $25 Production Credit
  • 10 completed paid collections: $75 Production Credit
  • 20 completed paid collections: $175 Production Credit

Monthly totals reset on the first day of each calendar month and do not roll over. Credits may be earned again each month.

Production Credits have no cash value, are non-transferable, and may be applied only to future eligible standard production fees while the Partner remains active and in good standing.

Signature Story Collection may correct errors, reject fraudulent or canceled orders, and update future reward structures upon reasonable notice.

15. Onboarding and Territory Activity Expectations

The first 90 days are an onboarding period with no sales requirement. The Partner may use this time to learn the offer, create the complimentary sample, add the service to the photography workflow, and begin introducing it to clients.

There are no mandatory monthly production purchases. After the onboarding period, Partners are expected to actively offer the service and should complete at least one paid collection per month to maintain exclusive protected territory status.

If a territory becomes inactive, the following process may apply:

  1. First check-in: Friendly outreach, review, and additional support.
  2. Second check-in: Partner Success conversation and a practical action plan.
  3. Final review: If inactivity continues, territory protection may be suspended or ended and the area may be reopened.

Signature Story Collection may consider seasonality, illness, emergencies, business changes, communication, good-faith marketing activity, and other reasonable circumstances before reopening a territory.

16. 90-Day Partner Success Guarantee™

A new Partner may request the published Partner Success Guarantee at the end of the first 90 days.

To use the guarantee, the Partner must personally speak with Founder Dan Rock, review the experience, provide honest feedback, and discuss whether any concern can be resolved.

If the Partner still decides the program is not the right fit after that conversation, the annual certification fee will be refunded in full.

Upon refund, Partner status ends, the dedicated Partner page may be removed, marketing rights end, and the protected territory becomes available to another photographer.

The guarantee applies to the annual certification fee only. It does not refund completed production work, third-party charges, client transactions, advertising costs, or other expenses.

17. Independent Businesses

The parties are independent businesses. This Agreement does not create an employment relationship, agency, franchise, partnership, joint venture, fiduciary relationship, or authority to bind the other party.

The Partner controls the Partner’s photography business, schedule, methods, pricing, taxes, insurance, employees, contractors, equipment, licenses, client agreements, and legal compliance.

Neither party may sign a contract, incur a debt, make a guarantee, or create a legal obligation in the other party’s name without written authority.

18. Confidentiality and Sensitive Information

Each party may receive nonpublic information about clients, projects, pricing, workflows, business methods, account details, marketing plans, or technical systems.

Confidential information must be used only for the Partner relationship and protected with reasonable care. It may not be disclosed except to people who reasonably need the information and are required to protect it, or when disclosure is legally required.

Confidential information does not include information that becomes public without breach, was already lawfully known, is independently developed, or is lawfully received from another source.

19. Intellectual Property and Project Licenses

Signature Story Collection retains ownership of its trademarks, logos, website content, Partner materials, badges, templates, forms, workflows, production methods, marketing resources, and proprietary business systems.

The Partner and client retain whatever rights they lawfully hold in their original photographs, logos, stories, and submitted materials.

By submitting materials, the Partner grants Signature Story Collection a nonexclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, crop, resize, arrange, caption, synchronize, host, display, and distribute the materials as reasonably necessary to produce, revise, deliver, support, and publish the requested collection according to the selected publication option.

Completed collections are provided for client personal use and the Partner’s ordinary client delivery and portfolio-related promotion, subject to applicable third-party platform and software restrictions.

No exclusive copyright ownership, copyright registration, work-made-for-hire status, or exclusivity is promised unless separately confirmed in a signed writing.

20. Suspension, Cancellation, and Termination

The Partner may choose not to renew at the end of the active term. Early cancellation does not create a refund except under the published 90-Day Partner Success Guarantee or where required by law.

Signature Story Collection may suspend or terminate the Agreement for:

  • Nonpayment or repeated late payment
  • Violation of this Agreement or the Terms of Service
  • Fraud, abuse, threats, harassment, or unlawful conduct
  • Unauthorized use of branding or Certified Partner status
  • Material misrepresentation
  • Submission of unauthorized or infringing materials
  • Continued territory inactivity after support and review
  • Legal, safety, platform, technical, or reputational concerns

Upon termination, the Partner must stop using Certified Partner branding and confidential resources. Outstanding balances remain due. Provisions concerning payment, confidentiality, intellectual property, indemnification, liability, and disputes survive.

Signature Story Collection does not guarantee sales, profit, client demand, territory value, search rankings, audience exposure, views, leads, or a particular creative reaction.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, total liability arising from a specific project will not exceed the amount paid to Signature Story Collection for that project. Liability arising solely from annual certification will not exceed the annual certification fee paid for the active term giving rise to the claim.

The Partner agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Signature Story Collection and its owner, contractors, service providers, and representatives from claims, losses, liabilities, damages, expenses, and reasonable legal fees arising from:
