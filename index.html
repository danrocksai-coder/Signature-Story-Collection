Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Answers Before You Apply

Does submitting the form reserve my territory?

No. Submitting the form begins the availability review. A territory is secured only after approval, acceptance of the applicable Partner terms, and payment of the annual certification fee within the stated time window.

How long is the protected territory active?

The initial certification term is one year beginning on the date the annual certification fee is received. Territory protection continues while certification remains active, paid, compliant, and in good standing.

Can I choose what I charge my client?

Yes. You control your retail price, deposit, payment schedule, and customer-facing refund terms. You keep everything collected above the applicable Signature Story Collection production fee.

How many photographs are included?

A standard collection includes between 1 and 20 finished professional photographs selected after the photographer completes the normal editing process.

Will you alter my photography?

We do not intentionally recolor, retouch, filter, or restyle your finished images. Minor cropping, scaling, or repositioning may be used only when necessary to fit the 16:9 video format.

Will my logo, watermark, or signature remain visible?

Yes. If it is already included on the submitted image, it remains part of the finished video. Qualifying public collections may also include a separate photography-credit end card.

Is every video public?

Public YouTube publication is the standard option when disclosed and authorized. Clients who prefer greater privacy must select the unlisted option before production begins.

What does unlisted YouTube delivery mean?

An unlisted video is not intended to appear in ordinary public search results, but anyone with the direct link may be able to view or share it. No internet-based delivery method guarantees complete secrecy.

Is AI used during production?

Yes. Premium licensed AI-assisted tools may be used for lyric development, music generation, editing, artwork, captions, and production support. Human direction, editing, quality control, assembly, and final review remain part of the process.

Is one revision included?

Yes. One reasonable revision round is included for corrections or limited changes such as a misspelled name, incorrect date, caption correction, reasonable image substitution, or minor timing adjustment.

What is not included in the standard revision?

A complete rewrite, replacement song, new genre, major video restructuring, extensive image changes, or work caused by omitted or incorrect information may require additional pricing or a new project.

Do I receive a complimentary collection?

Yes. Every active Certified Partner receives one complimentary standard collection during each active partnership year, subject to the applicable Partner terms.

Are there monthly minimums?

There are no mandatory monthly production purchases. Production charges apply only when you submit paid client projects. After the 90-day onboarding period, Partners are expected to remain reasonably active to maintain an exclusive protected territory.

What happens if I do not complete any sales?

There is no sales requirement during the first 90 days. After onboarding, prolonged inactivity may lead to support outreach, review, and possible reopening of the protected territory if the Partner remains inactive.

How do Signature Rewards work?

Five completed paid collections in one calendar month earn a $25 Production Credit, ten earn a $75 Production Credit, and twenty earn a $175 Production Credit. Monthly totals reset on the first day of the next month.

When do I pay the production fee?

For the first five paid projects, the production fee is due within 72 hours after delivery. After five completed and successfully paid projects, a Partner in good standing may request Net 30 billing.

Who collects payment from the client?

The photographer does. Signature Story Collection generally bills the photographer, not the photographer’s client.

Is the $25 Founding Partner rate permanent?

The first 100 approved Partners keep the $25 annual certification rate while they renew on time and remain active and in good standing. If certification expires or is deactivated, reentry is subject to current pricing and territory availability.

How does the 90-Day Partner Success Guarantee work?

At the end of the first 90 days, the Partner may speak directly with Founder Dan Rock. If the Partner still believes the program did not provide meaningful value and chooses to leave after that conversation, the annual certification fee is refunded in full and Partner status and territory protection end.

Are Partner pages guaranteed to rank in Google?

No. A Partner page creates a legitimate local business asset, but search visibility depends on competition, content quality, site authority, reviews, links, technical performance, and ongoing optimization.

Can I market this to past clients?

Yes. Past clients may be an excellent starting point, especially when you still have access to their finished professional photographs. The photographer retains control of the client list and sends the message from the photography business.
