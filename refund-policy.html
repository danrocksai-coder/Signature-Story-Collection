Refund Policy

This policy explains when refunds, production credits, cancellations, revisions, or other resolutions may apply to annual certification fees, Partner projects, and Signature Story Collection services.

Effective July 15, 2026

Signature Story Collection creates personalized, custom-produced digital work. Because songwriting, music generation, editing, captioning, artwork, and video production require time and creative resources, refunds are handled according to the stage of work, whether the service was delivered, and the specific guarantee or resolution terms below.

1. Policy Overview

90 Days Founding and new Partner certification guarantee period
1 Round Reasonable standard revision round before escalation
Case by Case Production issues reviewed directly with the Partner

Refund eligibility depends on whether the payment relates to annual Partner certification, a specific production project, an optional add-on, or a third-party charge.

Nothing in this policy limits any non-waivable right or remedy available under applicable law.

2. Annual Certification Fees

Annual certification fees secure approved Certified Partner status, access to Partner benefits, and protected territory status for the applicable term.

Except under the 90-Day Partner Success Guarantee™ described below, annual certification fees are non-refundable after the territory has been approved, Partner status activated, and program access provided.

Failure to use available Partner resources, market the service, complete projects, or generate sales does not independently create a refund right outside the guarantee.

If Signature Story Collection declines a territory request before certification is activated, no annual certification fee will be charged. If a payment was collected in error before approval, the payment will be refunded.

3. 90-Day Partner Success Guarantee™

A real conversation and a real 100% certification refund.

A new Certified Partner who concludes that the program did not provide meaningful value may request the published guarantee at the end of the first 90 days.

To use the guarantee, the Partner must:

  1. Contact Signature Story Collection at or near the end of the first 90-day onboarding period.
  2. Speak personally with Founder Dan Rock about the Partner’s experience, questions, concerns, and feedback.
  3. Allow a good-faith discussion of whether the concern can be resolved.
  4. Confirm that the Partner still wishes to leave the program after that conversation.

If the Partner still chooses to leave, the annual certification fee will be refunded in full.

After the refund, Certified Partner status ends, the dedicated Partner page may be removed, territory protection ends, Partner marketing rights end, and the territory becomes available to another photographer.

The guarantee applies only to the annual certification fee. It does not refund completed production work, client payments, payment processing fees, advertising expenses, optional purchases, business costs, or third-party charges.

4. Production Fees

Production fees compensate Signature Story Collection for the personalized creative work required to produce a custom-written song, cinematic photo story video, captions, artwork, and digital delivery package.

Once meaningful production has begun, the production fee is generally non-refundable except when:

  • Signature Story Collection cannot complete the project for reasons within its reasonable control
  • A verified duplicate or incorrect payment was collected
  • A legitimate production issue cannot be resolved through the included revision process
  • Signature Story Collection approves another resolution in writing
  • A refund is required by applicable law

Subjective dissatisfaction with melody, vocal tone, arrangement, style interpretation, pacing, or emotional response does not automatically require a refund when the project reasonably follows the submitted story and creative direction.

5. Project Cancellations

Cancellation Before Production Begins

If a project is canceled before meaningful creative production begins, Signature Story Collection may approve a full refund, partial refund, or production credit after deducting any nonrecoverable third-party charges or completed administrative work.

Cancellation After Production Begins

If songwriting, music production, video editing, captioning, artwork, or assembly has begun, payments are generally non-refundable because customized work has already been performed.

Cancellation After Delivery

After a collection has been delivered, the production fee is generally non-refundable. Eligible corrections or concerns should be handled through the included revision and resolution process.

6. Revisions Before Refunds

One reasonable revision round is included with each standard collection. Before requesting a refund based on a correctable production concern, the Partner should provide one clear, consolidated revision request.

Included revisions may cover:

  • A misspelled name or incorrect date
  • A limited lyric or caption correction
  • A reasonable image substitution
  • A minor timing or sequencing adjustment

A complete rewrite, replacement song, new genre, major restructuring, extensive image replacement, or work caused by omitted or incorrect information may require an additional fee rather than a refund.

7. Legitimate Production Issues

If a collection contains a legitimate production issue that cannot be corrected within the agreed scope, Signature Story Collection will review the matter directly with the Certified Partner.

An appropriate resolution may include:

  • A correction or additional reasonable revision
  • A partial production credit
  • A full production credit
  • A partial refund
  • A full refund of the affected production fee

The selected resolution depends on the nature of the issue, the work completed, the submitted instructions, the revision history, and whether the collection can reasonably be corrected.

8. Production Credits

A production credit may be offered instead of or in addition to a refund when appropriate.

Production Credits:

  • Have no cash value
  • Are non-transferable
  • May be applied only to future eligible production fees
  • Require an active account in good standing unless otherwise stated
  • May be subject to an expiration date stated when issued

Signature Rewards™ credits are governed by the separate reward terms published on the website and in the Partner Agreement.

9. Photographer-Client Refunds

The Certified Partner independently controls the retail price, deposit, payment schedule, customer-facing guarantee, and refund policy offered to the photographer’s client.

Signature Story Collection does not collect payment directly from the photographer’s client in the standard Partner workflow and is not responsible for refunding the photographer’s retail charge.

A Partner’s decision to refund a client does not automatically cancel or refund the production fee owed to Signature Story Collection.

Partners should avoid promising refunds, creative outcomes, or guarantees on behalf of Signature Story Collection beyond the written terms provided.

10. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Before filing a chargeback or payment dispute, the payer should contact Signature Story Collection and allow a reasonable opportunity to investigate and resolve the concern.

Filing an improper, fraudulent, or premature chargeback may result in suspension of production, removal of Net 30 privileges, suspension of Partner benefits, reopening of the protected territory, account termination, and collection of applicable chargeback costs.

This section does not prevent a person from exercising a lawful payment-dispute right.

11. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are generally returned to the original payment method when reasonably possible.

Processing time depends on the payment provider, bank, card issuer, and transaction method. Signature Story Collection cannot control how quickly a financial institution posts an approved refund.

Cash App, Venmo, PayPal, Stripe, card, bank, or other provider fees may be nonrecoverable when the provider does not return those fees. Any deduction will be disclosed when applicable and legally permitted.

12. Exceptions and Policy Abuse

Signature Story Collection may deny or limit a discretionary refund when a request involves:

  • Fraud, misrepresentation, threats, harassment, or abuse
  • Unauthorized or infringing project materials
  • Repeated misuse of revisions, guarantees, or chargebacks
  • Failure to provide required information or permissions
  • A request based on information the client or Partner omitted or supplied incorrectly
  • A request made after substantial use, publication, delivery, or distribution of the finished work

Signature Story Collection may still provide a reasonable correction or resolution when appropriate, even when a refund is not required.
