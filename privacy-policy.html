This Privacy Policy explains what information Signature Story Collection collects, why it is collected, how it may be used or shared, how long it may be retained, and the choices available to you. By using this website or submitting information through one of our forms, you acknowledge the practices described below.

1. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to information collected through SignatureStoryCollection.com, related partner pages, territory inquiry forms, future client story-submission forms, email, text messages, telephone conversations, project communications, and digital files submitted for a Signature Story Collection.

This policy covers information submitted by professional photographers, prospective and active Certified Partners, their clients, website visitors, and anyone communicating with Signature Story Collection about a project or business relationship.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Full name and business name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • City, state, ZIP code, and requested service area
  • Website, portfolio, and social-media links
  • Photography specialties and business information
  • Client names, relationships, milestones, memories, and stories
  • Music preferences, vocalist preferences, and creative direction
  • Details requested for inclusion or exclusion from a song
  • Photographs, logos, watermarks, signatures, and related files
  • Publication preferences, including public or unlisted YouTube delivery
  • Payment, billing, invoice, and transaction-related information
  • Questions, feedback, revision requests, and other communications

Information Collected Automatically

Basic technical information may be collected automatically by our hosting provider or other service providers when you visit the website. This may include an IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, pages visited, and approximate location derived from an IP address.

We do not currently use this website to collect sensitive financial account credentials, government identification numbers, medical records, or precise geolocation.

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Review territory availability and partner applications
  • Contact prospective photographers about the Partner Program
  • Create and manage Certified Partner accounts and local pages
  • Communicate about orders, projects, revisions, billing, and delivery
  • Create personalized lyrics, songs, captions, artwork, and videos
  • Identify the appropriate photographer and client for a project
  • Provide YouTube links, MP3 files, videos, artwork, and other deliverables
  • Apply photography credits, partner branding, and service-area information
  • Provide customer support and resolve production issues
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, or misuse
  • Maintain business, tax, accounting, legal, and transaction records
  • Improve our website, services, workflow, and Partner Program
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce agreements

4. Photographs, Stories, and Creative Materials

Signature Story Collection exists to transform a client’s story and selected professional photographs into a personalized custom-written song and cinematic photo story video.

Photographers and clients should submit only information, photographs, and materials they are authorized to provide. The person submitting materials is responsible for obtaining any permissions necessary for production, delivery, and the selected publication option.

Submitted photographs may be used to create the requested video, artwork, lyric materials, previews, revisions, and final delivery package. We preserve the photographer’s finished editing style. Images may be lightly cropped or repositioned when needed for the video format.

Public YouTube publication is used only when the applicable project information and permissions authorize public publication. When an unlisted option is selected and accepted before production, the video is intended to be accessible primarily through its direct link, although no internet-based delivery method can guarantee absolute secrecy.

5. How Information Is Shared

We do not sell personal information.

Signature Story Collection does not sell names, email addresses, phone numbers, client stories, photographs, or project information to data brokers or unrelated marketers.

Information may be shared only as reasonably necessary with:

  • The photographer or Certified Partner connected to the client’s project
  • The client or authorized recipient connected to the completed project
  • Service providers that support forms, hosting, email, storage, payment processing, music production, video production, and delivery
  • Professional advisers such as accountants, attorneys, insurers, or consultants when reasonably necessary
  • Government authorities or other parties when required by law, legal process, safety concerns, fraud prevention, or enforcement of an agreement
  • A successor organization if the business is sold, merged, reorganized, or transferred, subject to applicable legal requirements

Service providers receive only the information reasonably necessary to perform their role and may maintain their own privacy policies and security practices.

6. Third-Party Services

Signature Story Collection uses or may use third-party services to operate the website and deliver projects. These may include:

  • Netlify for website hosting, deployment, domain services, and related technical operations
  • Formspree for receiving website form submissions
  • Google Fonts for website typography
  • YouTube and Google for video hosting, publication, viewing, and delivery
  • Payment providers such as Stripe, PayPal, Cash App, or Venmo when selected for a transaction
  • Email, cloud-storage, music, video, and creative software providers used to communicate, produce, store, review, and deliver projects

When you interact with a third-party service, that provider may collect or process information under its own terms and privacy policy. Signature Story Collection does not control every action taken by an independent third-party provider.

7. Cookies, Analytics, and Similar Technologies

The current website does not intentionally use advertising cookies or behavioral advertising trackers operated by Signature Story Collection.

Hosting providers, embedded services, form services, fonts, video platforms, or other third-party tools may use cookies, logs, pixels, or similar technologies for security, functionality, fraud prevention, performance, or their own service operations.

If Signature Story Collection later adds analytics, advertising, remarketing, or additional tracking technologies, this policy and any required consent controls will be updated before or when those tools are implemented.

You may control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain technologies may affect website functionality.

8. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including project production, delivery, customer support, partner administration, billing, accounting, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, legal compliance, and enforcement of agreements.

Territory inquiries that do not result in a partnership may be retained for follow-up, recordkeeping, and future availability discussions unless deletion is requested and no legal or operational reason requires continued retention.

Project files and completed deliverables may be retained to support downloads, corrections, revisions, replacement delivery, portfolio records, and business continuity. Retention periods may vary based on the project, storage provider, publication status, and legal requirements.

When information is no longer reasonably needed, we may delete, anonymize, or securely dispose of it. Backup copies may remain for a limited period until overwritten through normal system processes.

9. Information Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no website, email account, cloud-storage platform, payment system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. You should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information and should use secure file-sharing methods when instructed.

10. Your Choices and Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable law and reasonable identity verification, you may request to:

  • Know whether we maintain personal information about you
  • Access or receive a copy of certain personal information
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete information that is no longer required
  • Withdraw consent for future optional uses
  • Stop receiving nonessential promotional communications
  • Ask questions about how information has been used or shared

Some requests may be limited when information must be retained to complete a project, process payment, maintain transaction records, protect legal rights, prevent fraud, comply with law, or preserve information already published with authorization.

To unsubscribe from promotional email, reply with “unsubscribe” or use any available unsubscribe link. Transactional, project, billing, security, and partnership communications may still be sent when necessary.

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under applicable state privacy laws. Depending on the law and whether it applies to Signature Story Collection, these rights may include requests to know, access, correct, or delete personal information and to receive information about certain disclosures.

Signature Story Collection does not sell personal information and does not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are commonly used in California privacy law.

We will not discriminate against a person for submitting a lawful privacy request. We may need to verify identity and authority before completing a request. An authorized agent may submit a request when permitted by law, subject to appropriate verification.

California customers may also request information about certain disclosures of personal information to third parties for their own direct-marketing purposes. We do not disclose customer personal information to unrelated third parties for their own direct marketing.

Browser-based privacy signals, including Global Privacy Control, will be evaluated and honored when legally required and technically applicable to the services in use.

12. Children’s Privacy

This website and Partner Program are intended for adults and professional businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without appropriate parental or legal-guardian authorization.

A parent, guardian, photographer, or authorized adult may submit photographs or story details involving a child for a legitimate family or client project. The submitting adult is responsible for having the authority and permissions required to provide those materials.

If you believe a child submitted personal information directly without appropriate authorization, contact us so the situation can be reviewed.

13. International Visitors

Signature Story Collection is operated from the United States. Information submitted from another country may be processed, stored, or accessed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

By submitting information, you understand that privacy laws and data protections may differ from those in your country. Where additional rights apply, requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the website, Partner Program, production workflow, service providers, or legal requirements change.

The revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes may also be communicated through the website, email, or another reasonable method when appropriate.
